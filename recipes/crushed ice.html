Crushed Iced Coffee

Crushed iced coffee is a delicious and refreshing summer drink. It's easy to make and only requires a few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp of coffee
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of milk
  • Your favorite drizzling sauce (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a cup, mix water and coffee.
  2. Freeze coffee ice cubes in an ice cube tray overnight.
  3. Crush the coffee ice cubes in a blender or food processor.
  4. Add milk and your favorite drizzling sauce to the crushed ice and enjoy!

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use cold brew coffee instead of regular coffee.
  • If you don't have a blender or food processor, you can crush the coffee ice cubes in a resealable bag using a rolling pin.
  • Get creative with your drizzling sauces! Try using caramel sauce, chocolate sauce, or even fruit syrups.
