Hot Dalgona

Dalgona coffee is a whipped coffee drink that is popular in South Korea. It is made with instant coffee, sugar, and water, and is topped with milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup warm milk

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the instant coffee, sugar, and water until frothy.
  2. Pour the milk into a cup and top with the whipped coffee.

Tips

  • Use fine-ground instant coffee. This will help to create a smooth and creamy foam.
  • Use hot water, but not boiling. Boiling water will make the foam collapse.
  • Whisk the coffee and water vigorously for at least 5 minutes. This will help to create a thick and stable foam.
  • Add your favorite toppings to your hot dalgona, such as whipped cream, chocolate shavings, or cinnamon.

Enjoy your dalgona!
