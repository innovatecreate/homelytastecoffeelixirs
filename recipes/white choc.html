White Chocolate Shaken Espresso

White chocolate shaken espresso is a delicious and refreshing coffee drink that is perfect for a hot summer day. It is made with white chocolate, espresso, and ice, and is shaken until it is frothy and cold. The white chocolate syrup adds a sweet and creamy flavor to the espresso, and the ice makes it the perfect drink to cool you down.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons white chocolate chips
  • Few drops vanilla syrup
  • 2 teaspoons water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk

Instructions

  1. In a small cup, combine the white chocolate chips, vanilla syrup, and water. Microwave for 30 seconds, or until melted and smooth.
  2. In a shaker glass, combine the white chocolate mixture, coffee, ice, and milk. Shake vigorously until frothy and cold.
