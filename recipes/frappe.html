Greek Frappe Ice Cream Float

Greek frappe is a coffee-lover's dream. It's frothy, creamy, and refreshing, and it's perfect for any time of day. But what makes Greek frappe so special?

It's all about the technique. Greek frappe is made by whipping instant coffee, water, and ice together until frothy. Then, milk is added to create a rich and creamy drink.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee
  • Â½ teaspoon sugar
  • 2 teaspoons water
  • Ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • Scoop of vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. In a cup, add coffee, sugar, and water. Froth until smooth.
  2. Add some ice cubes.
  3. Now, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  4. Lastly, add milk.
  5. Garnish with cocoa powder.

Enjoy!
