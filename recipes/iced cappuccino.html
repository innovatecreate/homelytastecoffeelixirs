Iced French Vanilla Cappuccino

Iced cappuccino is a delicious and refreshing coffee drink that is perfect for a hot summer day. It is made with espresso, milk, and ice, and is often topped with whipped cream and chocolate shavings.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ice
  • Whipped cream and chocolate shavings, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso.
  2. Pour the espresso into a chilled glass.
  3. Add the milk and ice to the glass and stir until combined.
  4. Top with whipped cream and chocolate shavings, if desired.
  5. Savor the icy goodness

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use whole milk instead of skim milk.
  • To make a frappuccino-style iced cappuccino, blend the espresso, milk, and ice in a blender until smooth.
  • For a more flavorful iced cappuccino, add a pump of vanilla syrup or chocolate syrup to the glass before adding the espresso and milk.
