Spanish Latte

The Spanish latte is a delicious and creamy coffee drink that is perfect for any time of day. It is made with espresso, steamed milk, and condensed milk. The condensed milk gives the latte a rich and sweet flavor that is sure to please.

The Spanish latte is a popular drink in Spain and other parts of Latin America. It is a great way to enjoy a delicious and refreshing coffee drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • A few drops of vanilla extract
  • Ice cubes
  • 2 teaspoons of coffee
  • 2 teaspoons of water

Instructions

  1. Make the Spanish latte sauce: In a jar, combine the condensed milk, milk, and vanilla extract. Cover the jar with a lid and shake vigorously until the ingredients are well combined. Set aside.
  2. In a cup, combine the coffee and water. Stir until the coffee is dissolved.
  3. Add 2-3 teaspoons of the Spanish latte sauce to the coffee cup. Stir until combined.
  4. Add ice cubes to the coffee cup and stir until melted.
  5. Add the remaining cup of milk to the coffee cup and stir until combined.

Enjoy your Spanish latte!
