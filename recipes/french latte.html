French Toast Latte

This delectable French toast latte is a delicious and refreshing way to start your day. It is made with maple syrup, milk, ice, and espresso, and is topped with a frothy foam.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1 cup cold whole milk
  • Ice cubes
  • 2 shots espresso

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together the maple syrup and milk until frothy.
  2. Fill a glass with ice cubes.
  3. Pour the espresso shots into the glass.
  4. Top with the maple syrup-milk foam.
  5. Mix well and savor the deliciousness!

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use whole milk instead of skim milk.
  • To make a frappuccino-style French toast latte, blend the maple syrup-milk foam, ice cubes, and espresso shots in a blender until smooth.
  • For a more flavorful French toast latte, add a pinch of nutmeg to the maple syrup-milk foam before topping the iced latte.

Savor the deliciousness of this French toast latte and may it bring you a smile!
