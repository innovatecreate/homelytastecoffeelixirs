Cinnamon Dolce Iced Latte

This delicious and refreshing iced latte is made with espresso, milk, ice, and cinnamon dolce syrup. It is often topped with whipped cream and cinnamon sprinkles.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ice
  • 1 pump cinnamon dolce syrup
  • Whipped cream and cinnamon sprinkles, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso.
  2. Pour the espresso into a chilled glass.
  3. Add the milk and ice to the glass and stir until combined.
  4. Pump the cinnamon dolce syrup into the glass and stir again.
  5. Top with whipped cream and cinnamon sprinkles, if desired.
  6. Treat yourself to a delicious cinnamon dolce iced latte and let your taste buds rejoice!

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use whole milk instead of skim milk.
  • To make a frappuccino-style cinnamon dolce iced latte, blend the espresso, milk, ice, and cinnamon dolce syrup in a blender until smooth.
  • For a more flavorful cinnamon dolce iced latte, add a pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg to the glass before adding the espresso and milk.
