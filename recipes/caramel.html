Caramel Frappuccino

Treat yourself to a delicious caramel frappuccino and let your taste buds dance in delight!

This rich and creamy frappuccino is made with coffee, milk, ice, and caramel syrup. It is then topped with whipped cream, caramel drizzle, and cookie chunks for a decadent and satisfying treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup ice
  • 1 pump caramel syrup
  • Whipped cream, caramel drizzle, and cookie chunks, for topping

Instructions

  1. Brew the espresso.
  2. Pour the espresso into a blender.
  3. Add the milk, ice, and caramel syrup to the blender.
  4. Blend until smooth.
  5. Pour the frappuccino into a cup.
  6. Top with whipped cream, caramel drizzle, and cookie chunks.
  7. Savor the sweet and creamy goodness of this caramel frappuccino!

Tips

  • For a richer flavor, use whole milk instead of skim milk.
  • To make a vegan caramel frappuccino, use plant-based milk and vegan whipped cream.
  • For a more flavorful frappuccino, add a pinch of cinnamon or nutmeg to the blender before blending.
